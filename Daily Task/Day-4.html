Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is an American automotive and clean energy company headquartered in Austin,
 Texas. Best known for pioneering the (EV)market, the company's mission
is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy through a vertically integrated
ecosystem of cars, solar generation, and battery storage

  1. Automotive: Designs, manufactures, and sells a lineup of all-electric vehicles including
    the Model 3, Model Y, Model S, Model X, and the Cybertruck.
  2. Energy: Develops and installs solar panels and large-scale battery storage, primarily
    residential solutions like the
    Powerwall and commercial systems like the Megapack.
  3. Charging Infrastructure: Operates the global Tesla Supercharger Network, allowing
    drivers to rapidly charge their vehicles on long-distance trips
